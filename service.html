Our Services

At Pwani Aqua Services, we are dedicated to delivering top-tier water and sanitation services. Our expertise spans water quality testing, wastewater treatment, infrastructure development, and emergency response. We ensure compliance with regulations, promote water conservation, and engage with communities. With cutting-edge technology and a commitment to sustainability, we prioritize public health and environmental impact. Trust us for reliable, cost-effective solutions, excellent customer service, and a healthier future for all.

Sewage Treatment

This service focuses on treating and purifying wastewater and sewage to remove contaminants and pollutants. It ensures that the discharged water meets environmental standards, minimizing the impact on natural ecosystems and public health.

Clean Water Supply

The provision of safe and potable water to communities is at the core of this service. It involves sourcing, treating, and distributing clean water to meet the daily needs of households and businesses, promoting health and well-being.

Community Clean-ups

This service involves organizing and executing initiatives to clean and improve the overall hygiene and aesthetics of local neighborhoods. It fosters community pride and environmental sustainability by removing litter and beautifying public spaces.

Drilling Boreholes

Borehole drilling is a vital service that involves creating access to underground water sources. It provides communities with a sustainable and reliable supply of clean groundwater, particularly in areas with limited access to surface water, ensuring water security and self-sufficiency.
